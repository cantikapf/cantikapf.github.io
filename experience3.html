003 : Lembaga Pembiayaan Ekspor Indonesia



Lembaga Pembiayaan Ekspor Indonesia (LPEI), also known as the Indonesian Export Financing Institution or Indonesia Eximbank, is a crucial entity that plays a pivotal role in supporting the country's export activities. Established to enhance Indonesia's global trade competitiveness, LPEI provides various financial products and services tailored to assist local businesses in their export ventures. These offerings include export credit insurance, financing solutions, and guarantees that mitigate risks associated with international trade. By facilitating access to funding and reducing the uncertainties related to cross-border transactions, LPEI contributes significantly to the growth of Indonesia's exports and its overall economic development.

Source: InfoBrand.id


During my internship at Lembaga Pembiayaan Ekspor Indonesia in 2023, I had the invaluable opportunity to contribute to the Communication Division's endeavors. One of my key roles involved collaborating closely with the skilled Communication Team to successfully accomplish the translation project for the company website. This task allowed me to enhance my linguistic skills while aiding in making vital information accessible to a wider audience. Additionally, I actively participated in the 'LPEI Goes to Campus' initiative, where I played a role in engaging with over 50 students from Bina Nusantara University. Through this project, I not only promoted the company but also gained hands-on experience in creating connections and fostering relationships within an academic setting. Overall, my internship experience equipped me with practical communication skills and an understanding of effective outreach strategies in a real-world context.
